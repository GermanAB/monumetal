Introducing ALUTEC.

ACP ALUTEC is a fantastic material composed of two excellent aluminum sheets attached to a low-density polethylene core with a fire-retardant (FR) material, with PVDF-Kynar Paint that gives you a color life of up to 20 years, exposed to ultraviolet rays.

It offers you more than 44 line colors for your construction projects like houses, shops, buildings, hospitals, educational centers, industries, shopping centers, among others.
